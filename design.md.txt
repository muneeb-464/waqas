## Brand & Style

The design system is engineered to project an image of technical mastery, premium craftsmanship, and high-performance engineering. It targets high-end tech recruiters and potential clients who value precision and modern aesthetics. The emotional response is one of immersion and sophistication—a "night mode" executive experience that feels both powerful and inviting.

The visual style is a hybrid of **Minimalism** and **Glassmorphism**. It utilizes heavy whitespace (or "darkspace") to allow content to breathe, while employing frosted glass layers and subtle radial gradients to create a sense of three-dimensional depth within a digital canvas. The aesthetic is punctuated by aggressive, high-contrast crimson accents that guide the eye toward key actions and narrative milestones.

## Colors

The palette is anchored in a monochromatic dark foundation to minimize cognitive load and maximize the "pop" of the primary accent. 

- **Primary:** Ruby Red (#E63946) is used sparingly but impactfully for primary CTAs, active states, and critical decorative highlights.
- **Surface:** The background uses a tiered approach with Deep Black (#0A0A0A) for the base and Charcoal (#121212) for elevated containers.
- **Gradients:** Use subtle radial gradients (e.g., a faint ruby glow in the background corners) to prevent the dark interface from appearing flat.
- **Glass:** Transparent layers should use a 60-80% opacity version of the charcoal background with a 12px-20px backdrop blur.

## Typography

This design system relies on **Plus Jakarta Sans** for its clean, geometric, yet friendly personality. The typographic hierarchy is steep, with massive headlines to establish a clear narrative flow.

- **Headlines:** Use tight letter spacing and bold weights to create a "tech-editorial" look.
- **Accents:** Key phrases within headlines can be colored in the primary ruby red or given a subtle text-shadow glow.
- **Readability:** Body text should maintain a healthy line height (1.6) and use a light grey neutral color to reduce eye strain against the black background.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a **Fixed Grid** model for the main content area to maintain a high-end, curated feel.

- **Grid:** A 12-column grid with generous 24px gutters. 
- **Rhythm:** Vertical rhythm is extremely spacious, with section paddings of 120px or more to separate different parts of the portfolio.
- **Alignment:** Central alignment is used for hero sections and contact calls-to-action, while project grids and experience timelines utilize asymmetrical arrangements to keep the user engaged.

## Elevation & Depth

Hierarchy is established through **Glassmorphism** and **Tonal Layering** rather than traditional shadows.

- **The Base:** The deepest layer is the #0A0A0A background.
- **The Glass Layer:** Cards and navigation bars sit on top with a #121212 background at 70% opacity, featuring a 1px border (low-opacity white or ruby) to define edges.
- **Blur:** A 16px backdrop-filter blur is mandatory for all floating elements.
- **Glows:** Instead of drop shadows, use "Outer Glows" using the primary ruby color at very low opacities (5-10%) to make interactive elements feel electrified.

## Shapes

The design system utilizes a medium roundedness to balance the "sharpness" of the developer aesthetic with a modern, premium feel.

- **Standard Radius:** 0.5rem (8px) for buttons and small input fields.
- **Large Radius:** 1.5rem (24px) for cards and main container wrappers to create a softer, more sophisticated silhouette.
- **Interactive Elements:** Buttons should feel substantial; use the standard radius but ensure the padding is generous (at least 12px 24px).

## Components

- **Buttons:**
  - *Primary:* Solid Ruby Red (#E63946) with white or black text. Use a subtle inner-glow on hover.
  - *Secondary:* Glassmorphic background with a 1px white border at 20% opacity.
- **Cards:** Feature a 1px top-down gradient border to simulate a light source hitting the top edge. Cards should have a "glass" feel with background blur enabled.
- **Inputs:** Darker than the container (#0A0A0A), with a 1px border that turns Ruby Red upon focus. Labels should use the `label-sm` typographic style.
- **Chips/Badges:** Small, pill-shaped elements with a faint Ruby Red background tint (10% opacity) and solid Ruby Red text for technical skills.
- **Timeline:** A vertical line in low-opacity grey with Ruby Red nodes indicating significant career milestones.
- **Code Block:** A specialized container with a #050505 background, featuring syntax highlighting that incorporates the primary ruby color for keywords.
